Refund Policy - Galagadi Tours and Safari

At Galagadi Tours and Safari, we aim to provide exceptional travel experiences in Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe. This Refund Policy outlines how payments, deposits, and cancellations are handled.

1. Deposit Requirement

A 10% non-refundable deposit is required at the time of booking to secure your tour or safari experience. This confirms your reservation and allows us to begin planning your itinerary.

2. Final Payment

The remaining balance must be paid prior to the tour start date, as specified at the time of booking. Failure to complete payment may result in cancellation.

3. Cancellation by Customer

More than 30 days before the tour date: refund of payments made, excluding the 10% deposit. 15-30 days before the tour date: 50% refund of the total booking amount, excluding deposit. Less than 15 days before the tour date: no refund will be issued.

4. No-Shows

Failure to arrive on the scheduled tour date without prior notice will be considered a no-show and no refund will be provided.

5. Changes to Bookings

We will do our best to accommodate changes to booking dates or itinerary, subject to availability. Price differences may apply.

6. Cancellation by Galagadi Tours and Safari

If we must cancel due to unforeseen circumstances such as extreme weather, safety concerns, or operational issues, you will be offered a full refund including deposit or the option to reschedule.

7. Third-Party Services

Some tours include third-party services such as accommodation or transport. Refunds for those services are subject to the providers' policies.

8. Force Majeure

Galagadi Tours and Safari is not liable for disruptions caused by events beyond our control, including natural disasters, political instability, pandemics, or travel restrictions.
