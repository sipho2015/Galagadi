About Us

Welcome to Galagadi Tours & Safaris.

We create personalized journeys that match your pace, interests, and dreams. Galagadi, more commonly spelled Kgalagadi, is a term from the Setswana language and generally refers to a vast, dry desert region known in English as the Kalahari Desert.

Our service was created to remove the stress of planning while opening access to exceptional experiences through trusted relationships.

Vision

To create seamless, personalized travel experiences through expert planning and trusted partnerships.

Mission

We believe travel should feel effortless, comfortable, safe, and enriching.

Trusted Partners

About Themba

Born and raised in Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe.

My full name is Thembelihle Hopewell Moyo, born and raised in Victoria Falls. My tribe's name is Ndebele. We descended from South Africa in the 18th century, moving north under King Mzilikazi, a once trusted General of Zulu Nation King Shaka.

I am proud of my culture and the unique environment in which we live, and equally proud to share it with others to witness and learn. We hope that the experience enriches your life.

Our Story

Growing up in Victoria Falls.

Tourism and wildlife were part of everyday life. Becoming a tour guide felt natural, and over time that role became a meaningful way to share Victoria Falls and the surrounding regions as they are truly lived, not just seen.

Themba's expertise now extends across the border into Botswana, offering unforgettable Chobe safaris that complement the Victoria Falls experience. Every trip is shaped by local knowledge, cultural pride, and a genuine love for this corner of Zimbabwe and Botswana.
