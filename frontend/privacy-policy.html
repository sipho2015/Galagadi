Privacy Policy - Galagadi Tours and Safari

Galagadi Tours and Safari ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or book our services.

We may collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, and booking details; payment information required to process deposits and payments through secure third-party providers; and technical data such as IP address, browser type, and website usage data.

Your information may be used to process and manage bookings, communicate about reservations, send confirmations and travel updates, improve our website and services, and comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers such as payment processors, accommodation providers, and transport operators, or with legal authorities if required by law.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

8. Retention of Data

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill booking services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
